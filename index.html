Centre for Computation and Data Science Education, Faculty of Arts and Science, University of Toronto

Activities for 2024 -2026

Incentivize intermediate- and capstone-level undergrad and grad data science courses in the humanities, social sciences, and life and physical sciences. (e.g., graduate teaching fellowships, call for proposals for collaborative data science courses)

Provide specialized technology development, support, and training for CDS education (e.g. JupyterHub, AI educational tools)

CDS professional development for graduate students and faculty (e.g., programming, data analysis, data visualization, machine learning)
